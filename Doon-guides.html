About Me

Born and raised in Mussoorie, I know every nook and corner of this beautiful hill station. I can show you the best sunrise spots, cheapest (but delicious) eateries, and hidden viewpoints that most tourists miss.

Sample Itinerary for Mussoorie

1

6:00 AM - Meet at Library Chowk, Dehradun • Shared taxi to Mussoorie (₹120-150 per person)

2

7:30 AM - Lal Tibba sunrise point with local tea and snacks

3

9:00 AM - Hidden breakfast spot near Cloud End

4

11:00 AM - Mall Road walking tour with shopping tips

Safety Tips

  • Carry water and sunscreen -- the sun can be strong at this altitude
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes with good grip
  • Bring a light jacket even in summer -- it can get chilly
  • I'll introduce myself with my DIT student ID
